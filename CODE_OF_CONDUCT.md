# Code Of Conduct

This project should be a useful, respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional, constructive, and patient.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and responsibilities.
- Protect private, sensitive, and operational information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates these expectations, contact a repository maintainer or use the appropriate internal reporting channel for the organization.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
